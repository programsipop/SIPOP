About SIPOP

The Scientific Integrity and Publication Oversight Program ensures rigorous, transparent, and ethical research practices in biomedical sciences. Our mission is to elevate research standards by providing thorough review and guidance, minimizing bias, and fostering credibility in scientific publications.

How It Works

Submission

Researchers submit their studies and findings for initial assessment.

Review

In-depth review of methodology, statistics, and ethical transparency.

Guidance

Detailed feedback and recommendations for improving research quality.

Validation

Final assessment to ensure rigor adherence to scientific standards.

Ethical Disclaimer

SIPOP is a scientific governance and oversight program. We do not provide ghostwriting services, data fabrication, or guaranteed publication. Our role is to audit methodology, enhance statistical integrity, and ensure compliance with international reporting standards (PRISMA, CONSORT, GRADE). All final intellectual and ethical responsibilities remain with the authors.
